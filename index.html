All India Student – Young Professionals – Women in Engineering Congress is the annual hallmark event of the IEEE India Council. Founded in 2000, the AIS(YW)C has grown over the years to become the biggest event on the calendar for IEEE members in India. It is a conglomeration of inventors, professionals, entrepreneurs, visionaries and some of the greatest minds in the country. By bringing together amazing people from all walks of life, the AISYWC empowers its participants to be inspired, collaborate together and innovate for tomorrow. Every year, AIS(YW)C is held at a different venue giving students, from all over the country, a chance to gain the maximum benefit from it.

AISC'11

In 2011, AISC was held at Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi from 29 September to 1st October, 2011. This Congress was hosted by IEEE Delhi Section.

AISC'12

AISC 2012 was organized by IEEE Bangalore Section at REVA Institute of Technology and Management, Bangalore. It took place from 7th to 9th December, 2012.

AISC'13

IEEE Madras Section was chosen to host AISC 2013. The three day student congress, that was held at Amrita University, Coimbatore, attracted over 300 student delegates from various IEEE Sections all over India.

AISC'14

The opening ceremony of AISC ‘14 was on October 6, 2014. This congress was hosted by IEEE Gujarat Section at Nirma University, Ahmedabad. It took place from 6-8 October, 2014.

AISYC'15

Continuing the legacy and keeping up with the needs of IEEE members, in 2015, AISC evolved to become AISYC i.e. All India Student and Young Professionals Congress. AISYC was hosted by IEEE Kerala Section and held at Rajagiri School of Engineering and Technology, Kakkanad, Kochi from August 7-9, 2015. AISYC 2015 concentrated on 3 main themes: Entrepreneurship, Professional Development, and Volunteering in IEEE.
